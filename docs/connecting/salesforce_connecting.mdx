## Creating your connection URI

1. On the Bracket web-app, for either the primary or secondary source, select "Salesforce" from the drop-down.

<Example>
  <img class="rounded-md w-full" src="/img/salesforce_drop_down.png" />
</Example>

2. Enter your Salesforce credentials, then click "Next"

<Example>
  <img class="rounded-md w-full" src="/img/salesforce-2.png" />
</Example>

3. Select the table you would like to sync from the drop-down menu. 

<Tip>If you do not see your Salesforce tables immediately, wait a few seconds and refresh the page</Tip>
