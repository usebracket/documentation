## Authenticating Bracket via OAuth

1. On the Bracket web-app, for either the primary or secondary source, select "Hubspot" from the
drop-down.

<Example>
  <img class="rounded-md w-full" src="/img/hubspot_drop_down.png" />
</Example>

2. Enter your Hubspot credentials, then click "Next"

<Example>
  <img class="rounded-md w-full" src="/img/hubspot-2.png" />
</Example>

3. If you have multiple accounts, choose the one that holds the correct data and click "Choose
Account":

<Example>
  <img class="rounded-md w-full" src="/img/hubspot-3.png" />
</Example>

3. Confirm that Bracket can have permissions to the respective scopes and click "Connect app":

<Example>
  <img class="rounded-md w-full" src="/img/hubspot-4.png" />
</Example>

4. Start syncing:

<Tip>If you do not see your Hubspot objects immediately, wait a few seconds and refresh the
  page</Tip>
